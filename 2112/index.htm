Electronic Magazines: 2112

This interestingly-named magazine mentions in the introduction that they are "serious" text-file writers, meaning they'll be concentrating very hard on writing textfiles. What this translates to is that they release a lot of their old textfiles of poetry, stories, and philosophy, and only one file (012) tells you how to rip off JC Penney. Interesting work, overseen by someone named Mephistopheles.
