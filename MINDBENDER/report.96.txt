This is a compilation of all the warez-reports I released in the year of 1996
plus some extra information on the months I didnt release to get a overview on
which group that acctually won the year of 1996...

* attention * the ratings are made by multiple opinions from people in most
of the different groups. There might also be alot of errors in the release
list about what game needed fix etc. I did try my best...

* Only final release version will be rated
* Only releases within the 50 disk limit will be rated
* Only releases that works 100% will be rated (no beta/unregistered)
* Only releases in english language will be rated

The first working version that is 'properly' stripped will recieve score !

All releases will be rated in a 1-5 point score (1 = crap, 5 = excellent).
Trainers and "real" addons (extra missions etc. NOT sound or animations) will
be rated with one point.

Non-working games that are fixed within a 5 day period will be rated with
a lesser score for each fix needed. Fixes are not awarded with score...

* note * the defination of a properly stripped game is where unnecessary
animations/intros/outros/speech etc is removed. The game must ofcourse
still be playable (ie: Command & Conquer - Hybrid releases in 1995).

error notes manual:
dupe - some other group (or the group itself) released the game earlier.
no game - the release is no game
size limit exceeded - for the time being, the size limit was exceeded.
needed fix - the game was not properly cracked
not working - the game was not playable
kiddie game - kids games are not rated

Authors comments: Hybrid started out slow in the beginning of the year and was
                  almost taken for dead when they suddenly got back on their
                  feet and sweeped the scene-floor with a few releases / month.
                  Due to internal fights, Hybrid was forced to split up in the
                  month of march but still without US suppliers the group ran
                  smoothly throughout the year and ended up as the second best
                  group of 1996.

                  The people that held Hybrid together and kept it from dry out
                  was ofcourse ÔUK supply godÔ Mad Turnip and ÔSwedish LeaderÔ
                  Hoson who worked against the competition without giving in.
                  In august, Hybrid got backup from ÔDominatorsÔ own Animal and
                  Dogfriend who helped out on multiple x-mas releases...

                  To round it up, Hybrid did an excellent year and for being a
                  euro-based, non-US group they work extremely well...
