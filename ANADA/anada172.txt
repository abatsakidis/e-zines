 Prison of Souls

 Playing warden to my soul,
 I'm locked in a prison of my own devise.
 I live, I die, but death won't end me.
 The death of me will bring me life,
 And free me from my vile fate.


 Dream World

 Last night I wandered,
 In sleeps velvet vail.
 On dream crystal potions,
 Behind silken sails.
 Cold marble laughter,
 Is taking my mind.
 To a place of illusion,
 Spaceless in time.
