Electronic Magazines: Blisters On My Butt

A late 90's attempt to parody the popularity of the Hogs of Entropy by releasing a number of simplistic textfiles and chanting "Death to HOE".

They have a web page which proclaims this mission statement:

"BLISTERS ON MY BUTT IS OUT TO SPREAD LAUGHTER AND DESTROY HOE. We're on a mission to prove to the world the conspiracy behind the zine known as Hogs of Entropy (currently passing themselves off as Heliots of Ectasy, but always refered to as HOE). There is no revolution. There is no such thing as the freedom fighters. We've killed numerous HOE writers, but they refuse to adknowledge this. We will show you how several HOE writers are in fact, ARTIFICIAL INTELLGENCE.

At the same time, we're committed to releasing really bad quality text files that are to make you laugh and ponder the existance of reality in this so-called advanced age of technology."

A near miss.
