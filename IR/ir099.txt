A kiss of cool air glides down my backside
I can feel it flow over my body
Making every hair stand on its end
Shiv'ring down all the way through the very bone
Taking all warmth that ever had been near
Chilling my scalp to no feeling at all
Giving no mercy to those in need
Remaining as free as any could be
Dear, take me not further than this place here
Away from me, shall shed a many tear
