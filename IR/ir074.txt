Geek, Nerd, Dork, Dweeb
Xade
4NOV00

Classification does not denote mental capacity or intelligence in any field.



Dweeb:  someone that is so much a dork that they dont deserve the term 
'dork'

Dork: one who has no developed skill

Nerd: possible wanna-be geek; skill but no value for that skill

Geek: adept at a certain skill or possibly many skills, in turn, places 
value to that skill, appreciating and fully understanding the skill and its 
value.
