Syndicate Power Action (SPACT)

Part of the SOLJO family of zines, it describes itself this way:

Syndicate Power Action is a small news letter which pays tribute to the roots of the Soljo - frequently released with the ultimate goals of being totally insane, badly written but slightly amusing SPACT is SOL's contribution to the glorious 'Rag's Division' of the textscene - and perhaps the first FIRE AND FORGET EZINE. SOL FACT: Historically "SP ACTION" was the name of the weekly print zine ( a one page news letter) produced by SOL back in "The Day" (1994); the name revived for this project especially. SPACT is also designed to be more readable on palm/handheld and WAP devices than your average zine with a smaller column formatting - the wit and charm of Soljo, on the move.
