BAH now has a distro site, *nine* members, and more files! Wow!

The new members are:
Neko
IamXTC
Murmur
Stumpy
Kirshana
Slurpee

The distro site is not up as of yet, but another request for a site was
mailed to me just this morning, so that's good. Neko sent the BAHfiles to
ftp.fc.com. The site is of now Green Eggs 'n Spam. It will be up this
summer and will be the BAH WHQ.

I pondered the WHQ idea for a while. Another group (not mentioning names)
in this area has WHQ's, distros up the ass, members all over the country,
yet it hasn't put out a pack yet. BAH's done ten files already with no
sites!

I recieved "submissions" from six people today, and now they're all
members. The next few BAHfiles will be those files that were sent to me.
It's nice to have new and creative people in BAH. Three people only go so
far.

I was debating the idea of releasing "packs" with multiple BAHfiles in it,
but I decided not to. Making "packs" or "mags" isn't my type of group. I
like the regular unpredictability of BAH. I don't like groups where people
put work into a file, than have to wait weeks, maybe months before it gets
published in one huge "pack" that takes forever to read. When someone sends
something to me, it gets published. Quickly. BAH will stay the way it is,
one tfile bundled in a group. Maybe an ANSI or so in there, but that's it.

Thanks again to all who brought this new grotto of luck to BAH.
